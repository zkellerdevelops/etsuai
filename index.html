Celebrating the Artistic Heartbeat of East Tennessee

The Arts Initiative is an ETSU and community movement recognizing a 30-year dream to build a fine and performing arts center for ETSU students and the surrounding region.

Much more than a single building, the Arts Initiative includes scholarships, endowed chair opportunities and support for arts across East Tennessee.

Enriching Possibilities for the Campus and Community

A facility that helps dreams take flight

The Arts Initiative supports the design and construction of a professional-grade fine and performing art space. Teaching, performance, gallery and administrative space within will serve a wide breath of programs.

The 130,000-square-foot facility includes plans for:

  • An auditorium seating roughly 1,000
  • Classrooms for music, theater and dance instruction
  • Administrative space to support academics and performances

Lasting economic and cultural impact

Johnson City’s economic and cultural growth is directly tied to the Arts Initiative. The center will act as a hub for artists and patrons in the region, 45 percent of which travel from outside the region. Notable benefits include increased tourism revenues, added jobs and more opportunities to attract prominent traveling art exhibits and performance groups.
